Blake Bronson-Bartlett is associate editor at the Mark Twain Papers and Project (UC, Berkeley). He has taught a wide range of courses in US literatures at the University of Iowa and the Technical University of Dortmund, Germany. His research and writings mainly focus on the nineteenth-century US, though his interests in the history of the book and writing tools have led him beyond US borders. He is the creator/editor of Mabbott Poe, a digital resource on the papers of Poe scholar and editor Thomas Ollive Mabbott. He has participated in several collaborative translations from French, Haitian Creole, and German, in addition to independent translations.
